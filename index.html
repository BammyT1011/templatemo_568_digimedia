Cognitive Activities

  • Cognitive interventions have proven effective for maintaining and/or improving cognitive functioning in elderly people regardless of their initial cognitive status.

    There are different types of interesting cognitive activities that has be proven by experts to improve wellbeing of elderly people

    Playlist and Meditation Jigsaw Scrabble Brain yoga Reading fiction books

    From the above listed cognitive activities, you can choose any activity that interests you.

  • Playlist & Meditation

    If your elderly relative enjoys music, then playing an instrument might be something that they find particularly inspiring.

    Purpose of this Activity

    Whether this is a skill that they have had for many years, or they are a complete beginner, the challenges to their mental and physical dexterity will strengthen neural pathways.

    What are essential materials needed?

    You will need meditation music that helps you relax and a comfortable position.

    Tips on how to do playlist meditation.
    Stay in a very relaxing position Start out with just a few songs and work your way up to longer practice.​ Time your practice with the number of songs you choose. If you find yourself ‘thinking too much’, don’t beat yourself up over it.
    How long will playlist meditation take?

    Depending on your choice, ideally, maximum of 30 minutes should make a positive impact.

  • Jigsaw

    if you want to start with a simple activity to stimulate the mind, Try completing a puzzle. Whether this is a skill that they have had for many years, or they are a complete beginner, the challenges to their mental and physical dexterity will strengthen neural pathways.

    Purpose for this activity

    While this might seem basic, there are many benefits for elderly people, such as a slower breathing rate which aids in reducing anxiety and lowering blood pressure levels. Jigsaw puzzles can also be a great distraction to help slow busy minds.

    What are essential materials needed?

    Coffee or kitchen table , jigsaw pieces, trays or bowls.

    Tips on how to play Jigsaw puzzle work?
    Put the pieces into a tray. Spread out the pieces. Work on a coffee table. ​ Look at the picture before you start. Organize pieces by colours and separate shapes.
    How long will jigsaw puzzle take?

    Maximum of 40 minutes but depends on how long you want the activity to last for

  • Reading fiction books

    It is good to encourage elderly people that reading is an activity they can enjoy independently. If they can’t read, you could spend time reading with them or set up a recording of the book If you want to add something extra to the book readings, ask them to tell the story back to you once they have completed the story. This will help with improving memory skills, and it adds a bit of socialising to what is usually a solo activity.

    Purpose for this activity

    The aim of this activity is enhanced vocabulary and the ability to boost cognitive skills. Tips on how to read fiction books

    Sit on balanced chairs Get a book holder Read in quiet places Look into eReaders Read out loud Take notes of texts that interest you.
    What are essential materials needed?

    Comfortable chairs and tables, good books, pens and notebooks, tea and coffee.

    How long will reading fiction book take?

    It is important to set time to read and assimilate, on this basis you can read for a minimum of 30 minitues

  • Scrabble

    Scrabble is a true classic activity that can be enjoyed by elderly people .

    Purpose for this activity

    There are certain benefits for elderly people such as improving memory functions (thinking of words to use on the board), lowering blood pressure (because of the relaxing nature of the game) and can increase overall general wellbeing.

    What are essential materials needed?

    Tables and chairs, scrabble boards, letter racks and tiles, pens and piece of book.

    Tips on how to play scrabble game
    Set up the scrabble board, letter racks and tiles. Give everyone piece of book to keep record of points . Agree on the dictionary to use for the challenge. Pull the tiles in the bag and shake it. Decides who plays first.
    How long will meditation take?

    This should take 20 minutes.

  • Solitaire

    Solitaire is recognised as one of the most popular card games in the world, and it can be played in online or in person.

    Purpose for this activity

    Card games like Solitaire help to keep brains sharp. It helps in increasing and stimulate memory and thinking of elderly people.

    What are essential materials needed?

    If played in person, you need the cards but if played online you need an access to the online game platform.

    Tips on how to play the solitaire games.
    Understand the objectives of the game. Build layouts. Put remaining cards in a separate file. Leave room at the top for your four foundation piles of cards.
    How long will meditation take?

    This will depend on how long you want to play the game maximum of 20 minutes is reasonable amount of time.

Mindfulness Activities

  • Mindfulness is the state of being fully present. Mindfulness activities gives elderly people and caregivers solution for dealing with physical and emotional ups and downs of aging.

    In this section you find Mindfulness activities such as

    Brain yoga Mindfulness breathing Journaling

    that helps to empower elderly people.

  • Brain yoga

    This is one of the easiest physical activities, yet highly mentally challenging for elderly people.

    Purpose for this activity.

    Brain yoga aims to stimulate the brain using only your body to help improve your motor abilities (which will in turn, improve neural pathways) and improve hand-eye coordination, resulting in increased mental abilities.

    What are essential materials needed?

    No equipment is required, and it can be done anywhere you only need yourself!

    Tips on how to do brain yoga.
    Make a fist with your right hand and raise your thumb. Hold this position Then, make a fist with your left hand and extend your pinkie finger ​ Now try alternating between each hand
    How long will brain yoga take?

    The instructions might seem simple, but you’ll find it’s quite tricky. Stimulate the grey matter in the brain and gain muscle memory by trying this with different body parts and A maximum of 5 minutes is enough for this activity.

  • Mindful Breathing

    Mindful breathing is a practice available to everyone. It is an important way to establish a routine and to become more comfortable with a particular practice.

    Purpose for this activity.

    Mindfulness breathing is a good way to control anxiety or stress. 

    What are essential materials needed?

    This is a very basic activity as you only need yourself in a very balanced position.

    Tips on how to do mindful breathing.
    Lie down or take a comfortable seat, if you can. You can also do this while standing or even walking, focus on your breathing. If it is safe to do so, close your eyes. Inhale a deep breath through your nose or mouth for about four seconds Hold your breath for up to five seconds. slowly release your breath at the count of six or seven.. Pause for up to four seconds afterward.
    How long will mindful breathing take?

    You can practice mindful breathing first thing in the morning or whenever you feel stressed or anxious. It can take a maximum of 3 minutes as it is a very quick activity.

  • Journaling

    This is an act of writing that. It is also a way of expressing wisdom, ideas and sharing memories.

    Purpose for this activity.

    Journaling gives you an outlet for all the overwhelming thoughts swimming in your head. It helps elderly people embrace creativity, share significant memories, and keep the minds healthy and active.

    What are essential materials needed?

    Notebooks, pens, chair and table

    Tips on how to journal
    Write journal prompts. Get your thoughts onto a page Read it.
    How long will journaling take?

    This depends on choice as it can take up to 30 minutes or more .

Physical Activities

  • As an elderly person, physical activity is one of the most important things you can do for your health. It prevents or delay many of the health challenges that seem to come with aging. It helps the muscles grow stronger so you can keep doing your day-to-day activities without becoming dependent on others.

  • Sit and Stand Exercise

    Sit and stand exercise strengthens muscles needed for essential activities like using the toilet.

    Purpose for this activity.

    This exercise helps to strengthen muscles in your buttocks and thighs.

    What are essential materials needed?

    Chair

    Tips on how to do sit and stand exercise
    Sit toward the front of a sturdy chair with your knees bent and feet flat on the floor and your shoulder-width apart. Rest your hands lightly on the seat on either side of you ​ Lean forward and feel your weight on the front of your feet. Breathe out. slowly stand up, using your hands as little as possible pause for a full breath in and out. Breathe in as you slowly sit down. Breathe out. Breathe out.
    How long can sit and stand exercise take?

    You can do this for 10 minutes.

  • Dancing

    Many older adults may easily recall the steps to their favourite dances, and although perhaps not as light on their feet as in their younger years, a slow waltz is a great way of getting up and moving.

    Purpose for this activity.

    Dance helps in overall health of elderly people. It also stimulates the body and provide opportunity for social interaction.

    What are essential materials needed?

    Good music, comfortable shoes and clothes , good space

    Tips on how to dance.
    Choose a dance style of preference. Start slowly. Rest when you feel tired.
    How long can dancing take?

    Minimum of 30 minutes
